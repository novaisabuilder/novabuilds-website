# Nova Builds - Design Brainstorm

## Design Direction: **Cyberpunk Minimalism with Neon Accents**

### Design Movement
**Cyberpunk Minimalism** — A fusion of clean, spacious layouts with high-tech neon aesthetics. Inspired by modern tech startups and AI-forward brands, this approach combines stark simplicity with electric energy.

### Core Principles
1. **Stark Contrast**: Deep dark backgrounds (near-black) paired with electric neon accents (cyan, magenta, electric blue) create visual tension and energy
2. **Minimal Clutter**: Generous whitespace, single-column layouts, and intentional negative space emphasize content hierarchy
3. **Neon Glow**: Subtle glowing effects, neon text shadows, and electric gradients convey cutting-edge AI technology
4. **Precision Typography**: Bold, geometric sans-serif for headings; clean, readable sans-serif for body text

### Color Philosophy
- **Primary Background**: `#0a0e27` (deep navy-black) — represents the void of digital space
- **Accent 1**: `#00d9ff` (electric cyan) — primary call-to-action, neon glow
- **Accent 2**: `#ff006e` (electric magenta/pink) — secondary highlights, energy
- **Accent 3**: `#7c3aed` (electric purple) — gradients, subtle accents
- **Text**: `#f0f0f0` (near-white) — maximum contrast on dark backgrounds
- **Muted**: `#64748b` (slate gray) — secondary text, borders

### Layout Paradigm
- **Hero Section**: Full-width dark background with centered, large typography and a glowing neon gradient accent
- **Sections**: Asymmetric layouts with content on one side, visual elements (gradients, glowing shapes) on the other
- **Cards**: Minimal borders, dark backgrounds with subtle neon glows on hover
- **Navigation**: Sticky top nav with minimal styling, neon underline on active links

### Signature Elements
1. **Neon Glow Effect**: Text and borders glow with electric cyan/magenta shadows
2. **Gradient Accents**: Electric blue-to-purple gradients used sparingly for visual interest
3. **Geometric Shapes**: Subtle animated circles, lines, or abstract shapes in the background

### Interaction Philosophy
- Hover states trigger neon glow intensification
- Buttons pulse with electric energy on interaction
- Links underline with neon color on hover
- Smooth transitions (300-500ms) between states

### Animation
- **Entrance**: Fade-in with subtle upward motion (200ms)
- **Hover**: Glow intensification, slight scale increase (300ms)
- **Loading**: Pulsing neon glow effect
- **Transitions**: Smooth cubic-bezier easing for all state changes

### Typography System
- **Display Font**: `Space Grotesk` or `IBM Plex Mono` (bold, geometric, tech-forward)
- **Body Font**: `Inter` (clean, readable, modern)
- **Hierarchy**: 
  - H1: 48px, bold, neon glow
  - H2: 32px, bold
  - H3: 24px, semibold
  - Body: 16px, regular
  - Small: 14px, regular
